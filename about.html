About Carlene Keegan

Locally servicing the greater Charleston area, with referral partners worldwide

Hi, I'm Carlene Keegan, a REALTOR® with The Perrella Group based on James Island, SC. I genuinely love where I live — the marsh views, the laid-back pace, and the fact that I'm just minutes from both Folly Beach and downtown Charleston. My office is in Mount Pleasant, which means my commute doubles as one of the most scenic drives the Charleston area has to offer. Real estate has been a passion of mine for as long as I can remember — I was the kid pulling up listings online and dragging my mom over to look at them with me. Somehow, that early curiosity turned into a career I'm deeply grateful for.

Before joining The Perrella Group, I spent three years teaching in Charleston County. That chapter shaped how I show up for clients today — with patience, clear communication, and a genuine desire to help people understand the process. A naturally competitive spirit from years of playing sports means I work hard at the table and advocate strongly on your behalf, whether you're a first-time buyer, relocating to the Lowcountry, or simply ready for your next chapter.

There's something especially meaningful about helping someone turn a monthly rent payment into equity and a place they can truly call home. I've had the privilege of guiding many buyers through that milestone here in Charleston, and every closing still feels like a win worth celebrating.

When I'm not working, you'll find me outside soaking up everything Charleston has to offer, exploring a new restaurant with friends, or at home with my two dogs. A few things that make me, me: I'm a lifelong Billy Joel fan, I can't say no to pizza, pasta, or a good bagel, I have a soft spot for coastal cottage architecture, and — fun fact — I'm ambidextrous. My guilty pleasure is Bravo television, and my dream job (after real estate, obviously) is professional pickleball player. I'd love the opportunity to help you find your place in Charleston.
